Slammers Beach Volleyball
About Volleyball

The object of the game

  • Players try to ground the ball on their opponents side
  • Opponents try to return the ball without grounding it

Serving the ball

  • The right back-row player who serves the ball
  • The ball must clar the net into the opponents court
  • The team winning a rally point has the right to serve
  • Players rotate one position clockwise.

Hitting the ball

  • A team is allowed to hit the ball three times
  • A player is not allowed to hit the ball twice consecutively

Blocking the ball

  • A player is allowed to hit the ball consecutively when blocking the ball
  • A team blocking the ball does not affect the number of times they can hit the ball

The Rally

  • The rally continues until the ball touches the ground/sand
  • Or if a team fails to return it to the opponent's court or commits a fault

Scoring points

  • The team winning a rally scores a point (Rally Point System)
  • When the receiving team wins a rally, it gains a point

Winning the game

  • A deiding game will be won by the team that first scores 15 points
  • Each non-deciding game will be won by the team that first scores 25 points
  • The winning team wins with a minimum two-point advantage (no scoring cap)
  • The best of three or best of five games will win matches
